Rules & Regulations
General Rules
  • Only students, staff who are a part of CU Lavasa will have access to the web link for posting their products using their email address.
  • Transaction details for all exchanges must be maintained and recorded.
  • There should be a mutual consensus between buyer and seller for the exchange.
  • Greenvasa only offers a technology platform to facilitate the exchange, buyer and seller will agree both price and delivery amongst themselves.
  • The project aims at reducing, reusing goods bought, kindly keep the same in mind.
  • Once posted and approved, the post cannot be edited.
Rules for Buyers
  • Payment terms to be decided between seller and buyer.
  • Buyer should check the product thoroughly before making the payment. The seller is not liable to return the money once the payment is made.
  • Buyer should maintain the Transaction ids of the payments for future references.
  • Buyers should ensure that they check the products thoroughly; the university does not warrant, endorse, guarantee, or assume responsibility for any product or service advertised or offered by a third party through the platform.
Rules for Sellers
  • The seller must compulsorily provide a detailed description of the product they wish to sell.
  • Sellers should post genuine products only.
  • Sellers should not post products that are in bad condition.
  • Sellers are expected to quote a feasible and relatively lower price than the new productâ€™s price.
  • Sellers should remove their product post once it is sold.
  • Sellers are expected to check their posts in the website frequently to know if any buyer is interested.
  • Sellers should ensure that genuine details are provided for buyers to contact regarding the product.
